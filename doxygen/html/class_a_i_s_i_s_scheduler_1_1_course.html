◆ timeSlot

std::list<std::string> AISISScheduler::Course::timeSlot

The time slot of a subject (time in, to time out) in 24-hour time (e.g. ["0800", "1000"] for a 8-to-10 class).

Note
It is assumed that classes start not earlier than 0700 and not later than 2100. It is also assumed that the classes occur in 30-minute intervals (so a time of "0845" or similar is invalid).
